## Monitor Buying Guide: What Matters Most

- **Resolution:** 1080p works but 1440p or 4K is noticeably sharper at 27" and above
- **Panel type:** IPS offers the best balance of colour and viewing angles for productivity; VA has better contrast
- **USB-C with Power Delivery:** One cable to your laptop for video, data, and charging — hugely convenient
- **Ergonomics:** Look for height, tilt, and pivot adjustment — you'll thank yourself in six months
- **Size:** 27" is the sweet spot for most desks; 34" ultrawide if you multitask heavily; 32" 4K if you sit further away
- **Refresh rate:** 60Hz is fine for productivity; 144Hz+ matters for gaming, not for office work
